About

Justice Cow is the musical alias of Jessica Kion, a multi-instrumentalist and songwriter whose work turns personal grief and quiet joy into full-band arrangements. Her latest record, my dad died, was recorded in four days with producer Bradford Krieger at Big Nice Studios.

Her music blends acoustic foundations with rich, full-band arrangements — intimate and expansive, grounded and searching.
