About

Indie rock
from Providence.

Justice Cow is the project of multi-instrumentalist and songwriter Jessica Kion — indie rock for fans of Wednesday, Big Thief, and Sufjan Stevens. Based in Providence with roots at Berklee and a decade of touring across the US and abroad, she writes unflinching songs about grief, memory, and community.

A frequent collaborator across the Providence scene, Kion has just finished tracking her next full-length album, due later this year.
